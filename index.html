Biography

Minyechil Alehegn Tefera received the B.Sc. degree in information technology from Wollega University, Nekemte, Ethiopia, in 2014, and the M.Sc. degree in computer science and engineering from Symbiosis International University, Pune, India, in 2018. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the College of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, National Taipei University of Technology, Taipei, Taiwan. His research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, fiber sensor networks, free space optics, and optical communication.
